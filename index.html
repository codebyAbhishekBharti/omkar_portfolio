Here's who I am & what I do

Jai Shree Ram !!!

I am Omkar Vinod Patil. As a passionate Athlete, I thrive in the dynamic and intense world of this traditional Indian sport. With a strong foundation in strategy, teamwork, and agility, I have honed my skills on the Kabaddi mat. My journey in Kabaddi is marked by dedication, discipline, and a relentless pursuit of excellence. Whether raiding or defending, I bring tenacity and quick decision-making to each match. Beyond the arena, I am committed to promoting the spirit of Kabaddi and inspiring others to embrace the physical and mental challenges it presents. My love for the game fuels my ongoing pursuit of success and sportsmanship.
